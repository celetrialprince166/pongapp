ELO Rating System

Player rankings are determined by ELO rating — a skill-based system where rating changes depend on the relative strength of opponents.

Starting Rating 1000
K-Factor — Amateur 32 (< 1500)
K-Factor — Pro 16 (≥ 1500)
Pro Threshold 1500
Formula R_new = R_old + K × (S − E) where S = actual score (1 win / 0 loss), E = expected score
